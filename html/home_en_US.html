Searching WU Library Resources Search Scopes

The catalog has several collections, or filters (search scopes), that allow a user to search specific combinations of our collections.

WU Libraries + Summit

Search for materials held by Willamette University and Summit.

WU Libraries

Search for materials held just by Willamette University Libraries

Articles

Search just the electronic resources to which Willamette subscribes.

Course Reserves

Use the Course Reserves option under Library Resurces for items on reserve. You may search by course name, number, instructor, or by title.

Use the catalog as a tool for research, but don't stop here...

The catalog does not include all content from specialized databases to which the Hatfield Library subscribes. For discipline-specific searching, use our Subject Guides and Databases for your research. Use WorldCat to search for items held by libraries worldwide.
